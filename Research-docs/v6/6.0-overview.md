# 4DGS Avatar Generator — MVP Architectural Plan v6

> **Version**: 6.0 — Critical Audit & Corrected Architecture  
> **Date**: March 2026  
> **Status**: Research-validated plan ready for implementation team  

---

## Project Overview

We are building an **MVP feed-forward model** for generating 360-degree photorealistic 4D Gaussian Splatting (4DGS) avatars from sparse camera views (1-3 cameras). The system must generate a complete animated avatar in under 3 seconds, renderable at 60+ FPS on consumer GPUs (RTX 4090, 24 GB VRAM), and deployable as ComfyUI nodes.

**Training data**: Proprietary 90-camera studio rig, 4K resolution, 60 FPS, hardware-synchronized. Each 10-second capture session produces ~54,000 frames.

---

## v5 → v6 Corrections Summary

This v6 plan is the result of a critical audit of 12 v5 research documents. The audit identified **7 significant overstatements, 3 critical gaps, and 2 new commercially viable alternatives** that materially change the architecture. Key corrections:

| Area | v5 Assumption | v6 Correction | Impact |
|------|--------------|---------------|--------|
| Loss functions | Chamfer Distance absolutely impossible | Near-linear approximate algorithms exist; still impractical for training but not "impossible" | Changes reasoning, not conclusion |
| Sparse-view strategy | Diffusion at inference = dead end | 1-step diffusion and hybrid feed-forward+refinement achieve near-real-time | **Architecture change**: add refinement stage |
| LRM quality | Single-pass LRM produces flawless back regions | Pure feed-forward LRMs produce blurry occluded regions | **Architecture change**: mandatory refinement |
| Kinematics | DCT/polynomial trajectories sufficient for all motion | DCT fails for loose clothing, hair dynamics | **Architecture change**: add physics-aware GNN module |
| Data preprocessing | Diagonal FIM is optimal | Diagonal FIM questionable for heterogeneous parameters; Squisher (free from Adam) is better | Simplifies pipeline |
| Hardware | Must use Blackwell B200 + NVFP4 | NVFP4 has convergence issues; H100+FP8 is safer and 2-3x cheaper for MVP | **Cost reduction**: ~$57K vs ~$100K+ |
| Body model | SMPL blocked, no alternatives | Anny (Apache 2.0) and MHR (Apache 2.0) are drop-in replacements | **Risk elimination** |

## Section Breakdown

### [Section 1: Loss Functions and Graph Matching](6.1-loss-functions.md)
**Key decision**: Composite rendering loss (L1 + LPIPS + DISTS) with depth/normal priors. No point-cloud matching in training loop.

### [Section 2: Sparse-View Generation](6.2-sparse-view.md)
**Key decision**: Two-stage hybrid — voxel-aligned LRM base (~0.5s) + 1-step diffusion refinement (~1-2s). Total: <3s.

### [Section 3: Kinematics and Temporal Consistency](6.3-kinematics.md)
**Key decision**: Tiered motion — static (3D) / skeletal (DCT-12) / dynamic (physics-aware GNN). Soft learned segmentation masks.

### [Section 4: Data Preprocessing](6.4-data-preprocessing.md)
**Key decision**: Multi-stage filtering — geometric diversity → temporal subsampling → Squisher (free Fisher from Adam). Target: 65-75% data reduction.

### [Section 5: Hardware Economics](6.5-hardware-economics.md)
**Key decision**: MVP on H100/H200 + FP8. Optional B200 + NVFP4 migration for production scaling. Estimated training cost: ~$57K.

### [Section 6: Compression and Deployment](6.6-compression-deploy.md)
**Key decision**: MEGA DC-AC + opacity entropy pruning + P-4DGS temporal compression. Target: ~10-20 MB per 10s clip.

### [Section 7: Legal Strategy](6.7-legal-strategy.md)
**Key decision**: MHR (Apache 2.0) as body model, gsplat (Apache 2.0) as rasterizer, Clean-Room Design for transformer code. FTO search budget: ~$16K.

## Risk Register

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| LRM cannot produce acceptable occluded regions | Medium | High | 1-step refinement as mandatory second stage |
| DCT fails for complex clothing in production | High | Medium | GNN dynamics module (budgeted in architecture) |
| NVFP4 convergence issues block B200 migration | Medium | Low | Stay on H100+FP8 (already primary plan) |
| Anny/MHR ecosystem too immature | Low | Medium | SMPL-X topology compatibility provides fallback to SMPL ecosystem |
| Clean-Room process causes timeline overrun | Medium | High | Parallelize module development across 3-4 teams |
| ComfyUI VRAM budget exceeded | Medium | Medium | Aggressive compression (MEGA+P-4DGS); progressive loading |
| Patent infringement discovered during FTO | Low | Critical | Early FTO search; design around if needed |
